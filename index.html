Cats Blender Plugin

This website is ran by The Neoneko team which maintain the Unofficial Cats Plugin. This website aim is to make it easier for you to choose the correct version of Cats for your Blender version and to get help with your specific version of Cats.

Please select your Blender version from the dropdown menu below, and you will see instructions on how to download the appropriate Cats version for your Blender installation, as well as information on how to get support for your particular setup.
